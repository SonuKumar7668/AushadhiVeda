Tulsi

benefits

  • Tulsi is known to enhance the immune system, making the body more resistant to infections and diseases.
  • The plant contains various antioxidants that help neutralize harmful free radicals, protecting cells from damage.
  • Tulsi has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce inflammation in the body, potentially alleviating conditions like arthritis and heart disease.

Remedies

  • Tulsi Tea: Boil 10-12 Tulsi leaves in a cup of water. Add honey and drink this tea twice a day to relieve cold and cough symptoms.
  • Tulsi Leaves: Chewing a few fresh Tulsi leaves daily can help reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Tulsi Paste: Make a paste of Tulsi leaves and apply it to the affected area to treat acne, eczema, and other skin conditions.
